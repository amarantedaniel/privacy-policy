Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I unlock new name packs?
    Tap the pack selector at the top of the name screen, then choose a locked pack to purchase.
  • Where are my liked names saved?
    Tap the heart icon at the bottom of the screen to view all the names you've liked.
  • Is my data shared?
    No. We do not collect personal information or share data with third parties.
