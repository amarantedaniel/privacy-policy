Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I log my creatine?
    Tap the big button. That's it. You can also tap the button right on the home screen widget.

    You missed a day. The streak counts consecutive days — we both know the rules.
  • How do I stop the reminders?
    Take your creatine. Alternatively, revoke notification permission in Settings, but that feels like giving up.
  • Is my data shared?
    No. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no cloud. See the privacy policy.
