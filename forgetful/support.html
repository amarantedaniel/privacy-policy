Support – Forgetful

Thanks for using Forgetful! It keeps the one thing you need to remember pinned to your Dynamic Island and Lock Screen until it's done.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a reminder?
    Type what you need to remember and tap "Create Reminder". It appears in your Dynamic Island and on your Lock Screen right away.
  • Why don't I see my reminder in the Dynamic Island?
    Live Activities need the notifications permission. Enable it in Settings → Forgetful → Notifications.
  • Can I have more than one active reminder?
    One at a time — that's the point. Tap a different reminder in your history to switch to it, or tap the active one to turn it off.
  • How do I delete a reminder?
    Swipe left on any reminder in the history to delete it.
  • Is my data shared?
    No. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no cloud. See the privacy policy.
